Hard XSLT Transformation

The stylesheet below has rule conflicts. In general, in such cases the most specific rule has a higher priority and is applied first. For instance, a rule with match="*" is always the least specific, a rule with match="A[@B]" is very specific and has a high priority. You can read more about the conflict resolution for template rules here.

We oversaw that there is one unresolvable conflict in this stylesheet between the rules with match="C" and match="C|D". match="C|D" is equvalent to two rules: match="C" and match="D". That means that there are two rules in the stylesheet with match="C" which naturally have the same priority. The order in which they are applied is undefined (by the XSLT recommendation) and thus depends on the implementation of the XSLT processor.

Good news: There will be no stylesheets with rule conflicts in the exam.
